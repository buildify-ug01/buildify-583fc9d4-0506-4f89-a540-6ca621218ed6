How Deals Progress

5-Stage Sales Process

  • Discovery (Week 1) – Qualify fit, understand pain points, map stakeholders
  • Demo (Week 2) – Tailored product walkthrough, address specific use cases
  • Technical Evaluation (Week 3-4) – Proof of concept, integration review, security audit
  • Business Case (Week 5) – ROI analysis, pricing proposal, contract negotiation
  • Close & Onboard (Week 6-8) – Legal review, signatures, implementation kickoff

Average sales cycle: 6-8 weeks for mid-market deals
