Pricing Strategy Rationale

  • Value Metric: Priced on user seats + feature tiers (aligns cost with customer growth)
  • Anchoring: Enterprise tier creates premium positioning, makes Growth tier attractive
  • Land & Expand: Start with Starter, upsell to Growth as teams scale
  • Annual Incentive: 15% discount drives cash flow and reduces churn
  • Competitive Position: 20-30% premium vs. alternatives justified by superior ROI
