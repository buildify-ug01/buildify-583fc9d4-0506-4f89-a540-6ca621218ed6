Early Validation Tactics

Proving Product-Market Fit

  • Design Partners (Month 1-3): 5-10 beta customers, free access for feedback and case studies
  • Pilot Programs (Month 4-6): 50% discount for early adopters, 90-day commitment
  • Success Metrics: Track activation rate, time-to-value, feature adoption, NPS scores
  • Feedback Loops: Weekly check-ins, monthly QBRs, feature voting board
  • Conversion Signal: 70%+ pilot-to-paid conversion validates pricing and value
